About SoutulKhair

And from amongst you there must be a group of people inviting to all that is Good (Islam)

Our Vision

To globally expand and provide accessible 24/7 Islamic education, through the principles of Tasawuf and Sufism.

Our Mission

Deliver high-quality Islamic education under the leadership of Patron Abul Jamal-al-Shaykh Muhammad Badar Alam Jan.

Patron In Chief

Hadrat Khawaja Abul Jamal Muhammad Badar e Aalam Jan

Islamic scholar & spiritual leader

He has received extensive religious education, holds multiple IJAZAT AND KHILAFAT from renowned saints, and is deeply involved in spiritual guidance and religious tours globally.

He is actively involved in spiritual tours and Islamic teachings, working tirelessly to spread Islamic values and provide religious education. His efforts extend globally, impacting many lives and fostering spiritual growth.
