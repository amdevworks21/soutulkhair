Quran Education at Abul Khair Foundation

Our Legacy of Quran Education

Jamia Abdul Khair Foundation has been actively providing teaching of the Quran since 1981. In keeping with today's needs, we have expanded our services to include Online Quran Academy offerings. Our esteemed Quran teachers conduct classes on Nazrah, Tajweed, and Qirat. This service is particularly beneficial for Muslims worldwide who do not have easy access to Islamic centers in their own countries.

Flexible and Accessible Learning

Our main focus is to provide Quran lesson classes in a convenient and flexible manner for both kids and adults, right from the comfort of their homes. By offering online classes, we ensure that the basic and advanced knowledge of Islam is accessible to everyone, regardless of their location.

Get Started with Our Online Classes
